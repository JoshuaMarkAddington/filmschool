Acting for Screen

The Idea of Presence. Step in front of the glass and master the ultra-high-definition art of "the moment." In a 6K environment, every breath and eyelid twitch tells a story. We move beyond "theatrical" acting into the raw, cinematic realism required for modern 4K HDR films.

Musical Theatre for Screen

This is where Broadway meets the big screen. Learn how to translate the energy of live performance into a structured cinematic narrative. We focus on the precision of lip-syncing, movement for the lens, and integrating high-fidelity audio with sweeping 6K visuals to create "The New Hollywood" musical.

Scriptwriting

Developing a compelling script is the cornerstone of any successful film. In this module, students will learn essential techniques for creating engaging storylines and characters that resonate with audiences. Emphasis will be placed on character development, plot structure, and dialogue that drives the narrative forward.

Directing & Production Leadership

From concept to final cut, dive into the world of film directing. Learn key elements such as storyboarding, shot selection, and scene construction that are essential for crafting a cohesive narrative.

Cinematography & AudioVisual Production

Unlock the secrets of cinematography with practical lessons in camera operation and lighting techniques, preparing you to capture breathtaking visuals that enhance your film's storytelling.

Editing & Digital Mastering

A film is truly "born" in the edit. You'll dive deep into DaVinci Resolve, mastering the same tools used by Hollywood colourists. Go beyond basic cuts—use Fusion for advanced visual effects, learn HDR grading, audio mastering and use 1.5TB worth of cinema grade visual effects to bring the best out of the films you make.

Become A Supporting Actor

Through hands-on exercises and real-world experiences, you'll gain the confidence and expertise needed to shine in supporting roles. Whether you're looking to enhance your acting skills or simply want to try something new, this module is an excellent way to develop your creative horizons.

The Recording-Suite: Vocal Edition

Perfecting the art of sound is crucial to creating a truly immersive cinematic experience. In this module, learn how to use industry-standard recording software to capture high-quality audio that complements your visual storytelling.

Advanced Cinematography

Unlock the Secrets of Visual Storytelling... In this advanced course, students will delve into the art of cinematography, exploring the technical aspects of camera operation, lighting design, and visual composition.

Advanced Dance

Take your dance training to the next level! You will learn advanced dancing techniques and choreography, which will push you to become a true professional. You will have the opportunity to perform at the highest level.

Cinematic Lighting

In this cutting-edge module, students will dive into the world of cinematic lighting, learning how to shape stories, evoke emotions, and create visually stunning films. From natural lighting to advanced techniques used in Hollywood blockbuster, you will have the tools light every film perfectly.

Voice Acting & Foley Performance

This dynamic module is designed for students who want to bring characters and worlds to life through sound. From voice acting to creating immersive foley effects, you'll learn how to captivate audiences with your voice and creativity.
