Welcome to Adders Film School!

Opening in Milton Keynes in July

The home of filmmaking right in the heart of Milton Keynes. We provide a 3-month film production cycle where you create, film and produce your own films with us across the year.

  • Acting for Screen
  • Acting for Musical Performance
  • Improvisation & Devised Screen Performance
  • Scriptwriting
  • Directing & Production
  • Leadership
  • Cinematography & Audiovisual Production
  • Editing & Digital Mastering
This is where the start of your storytelling journey begins.

Joshua Mark Addington

Acting & Scriptwriting Teacher
Award winning short film director, writer and the founder of Adders Film School. He has received several awards for his work from several international film festivals. Some of his films include ‘Recollections: Part One’, ‘Movie Day’ and ‘Occupied’. Joshua hosted the first international film festival at Odeon in Milton Keynes and has been teaching for over 8 years.
